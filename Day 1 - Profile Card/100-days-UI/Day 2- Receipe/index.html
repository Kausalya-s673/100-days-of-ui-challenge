Gathering the Magic Ingredients

  • 2 cups of idli/dosa batter – thick and slightly fermented
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Curry leaves
  • ¼ cup grated carrot
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil

How to Make Kuzhi Paniyaram

Follow these easy steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves.
  2. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and grated ginger. Sauté till soft.
  3. Add grated carrot, cook slightly. Turn off heat and mix into the batter.
  4. Grease the paniyaram pan with little oil.Pour the batter into each mould.
  5. Pour the batter into each mould.
  6. Cover and cook on medium flame for 3–4 mins.
  7. Flip each paniyaram and cook until golden.
  8. Finally Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
